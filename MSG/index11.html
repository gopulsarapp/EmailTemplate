If Your Backups Can’t Do This,
They’re Not Real Backups

Many businesses believe they’re protected—until they actually need to restore data. That’s when the truth about their “backups” comes out.

Backup maturity: Stored → Tested → Recoverable → Resilient

Real backups aren’t just about storing data. They must be able to:

  • Restore quickly — not days later
  • Recover entire systems, not just files
  • Protect against ransomware and corruption
  • Be tested regularly, not assumed to work
  • Support business continuity, not chaos

If your backups can’t meet these standards, your business is one incident away from serious downtime—or permanent data loss.

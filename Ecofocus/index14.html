Simplifying ESG Reporting for Education Institutions in British Columbia

Education institutions across British Columbia are facing growing pressure to deliver transparent, compliant, and data-driven ESG reports—while managing limited resources and complex data environments.

  • ESG data scattered across facilities, HR, academics, and procurement systems
  • Difficulty aligning reports with GRI, TCFD, CSRD, and evolving sustainability standards
  • Complex carbon, energy, and climate-risk disclosures across large campuses
  • Manual reporting processes causing delays, inaccuracies, and compliance risks
  • Challenges in measuring DEI, student well-being, and community impact
  • Lack of clear ESG dashboards for leadership, regulators, and stakeholders

Ecofocus is an advanced ESG reporting platform built to solve these challenges. Using modern technologies such as Python, Apache NiFi, Superset, and Vector Databases, we help education institutions automate ESG data pipelines and generate audit-ready reports with confidence.

Our platform supports leading frameworks including GRI, CSRD, TCFD, and more—ensuring your institution stays compliant, transparent, and future-ready.
